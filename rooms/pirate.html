Pirate's Chamber

The Pirate Room - Heist on The High Seas

Ideal For

Birthday parties, corporate teambuilding, date night, bachelor parties, or just a fun night out with family or friends

Premise

In the year 1788, Blackbeard and his pirates have made a short stop at a local port and are drinking their cares away at a local tavern. One of his pirates has hired your team to sneak aboard his ship and steal a map to Blackbeard's buried treasure. The double-crossing pirate will stand guard at the door and watch for signs that Blackbeard and the rest of the crew are on their way back, all the while helping you solve the devious puzzles Blackbeard has set up in his captain's quarters to protect his map. With help from the pirate, you have one hour to collect all pieces of the treasure map and escape the ship, lest Blackbeard catch you and make you walk the plank!

Disclaimer

You may have trouble with this experience if you:
  • 1. have trouble seeing in dimly lit rooms
  • 2. have trouble reading small writing
  • 3. have trouble manipulating small objects such as locks
  • 4. Are severely claustrophobic
  • 5. Unusually sensitive to sounds, scents, or surprises

Assurance

  • 1. This is in no way scary
  • 2. If for any reason you need to leave the room, you will be allowed to
  • 3. You will not have to do any complex math, reading, memorization, or have any prior knowledge of anything to complete the room

               

The Pirate Room

You've snuck aboard Blackbeard's pirate ship. Find the five pieces of his treasure map and escape before he catches you.

  • Recommended Group Size: 4 to 8, but 2 to 10 allowed
  • Difficulty: 2 (74% of groups escape)
  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Price: $25 a person
  • Objective: Work together as a team to find five pieces of a treasure map and escape the ship in under 60 minutes.
  • Setting: The year is 1788. You are in the captain's quarters of Blackbeard the pirate, aboard his pirate ship while it sits at port.
