How It Works

  • Pick a Chamber

    Choose one of the secret chambers for your adventure. Currently, only the Pirate room is available but more rooms with different themes will be coming in the near future.

  • Objective

    The purpose of this pirate adventure is to find blackbeard's treasure map. It is hidden some where in the room

  • Search for Clues

    Search throughout the room for any clues you can find that will help you put the pieces of the treasue map together.

  • Don't get caught!

    Pirates will enter the room as your searching for clues. Don't let them catch you or you'll be out for the rest of the game.

  • Escape

    Escape by finding all the pieces of Blackbeard's treasure map before he and his men return.


  • Have fun!

FAQ

  • LENGTH OF GAME

    You have exactly 45 minutes to escape the room.

  • NUMBER OF PPL

    Most of our theme rooms are designed for an optimal group of four or more players. Fewer players are most welcome, but keep in mind it may be more difficult!

  • AGE + HEALTH

    Some of our rooms do have restricted themes so when it comes to these we do ask that players be in good health and at least 18 years of age.

  • WALK-IN

    Yes, however we cannot guarantee full availability of our theme rooms. We recommend that you book in advance.

  • ARRIVAL TIME

    We recommend that players arrive at least 5 minutes prior to the booked time for a briefing. If you are late, we will not extend your time and your playing time may be reduced.

  • WHAT TO BRING

    Members, please bring your membership cards for your discount. Otherwise, just bring yourselves!
