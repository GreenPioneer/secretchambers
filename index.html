How Our Escape Room Works

This is real-life escape room games are a type of physical adventure game in which people are locked in a room with other participants and have to use elements of the room to solve a series of puzzles, find clues, and escape the room within a set time limit.

  • Pick a Chamber

    Choose one of the secret chambers for your adventure. Currently, only the Pirate room is available but more rooms with different themes will be coming in the near future.

  • Objective

    The purpose of this pirate adventure is to find blackbeard's treasure map. It is hidden some where in the room

  • Search for Clues

    Search throughout the room for any clues you can find that will help you put the pieces of the treasue map together.

  • Don't get caught!

    Pirates will enter the room as your searching for clues. Don't let them catch you or you'll be out for the rest of the game.

  • Escape

    Escape by finding all the pieces of Blackbeard's treasure map before he and his men return.


  • Have fun!

FAQ

  • How many people?

    5-8 people per room per session. If you have less than 5 people give us a call and we'll try to make accommodations.

  • special session

    Have more than 8 people? Want to participate outside our normal hours? We love special events, give us a call and we'll try to make accomodations

  • AGE + HEALTH

    We recommend ages 16 and up and all groups must have at least person over 21. No children under 6 will be admitted.

  • DIFFICULTY

    We provide a challenging but fair experience that requires no special skills other than teamwork. We guarantee you'll have a lot of fun.

  • ARRIVAL TIME

    Please arrive at least 15 minutes early. If you're late and the session behind yours is booked, we must deduct the time from your session.

  • What if I get stuck?

    If you signal us, we'll give you a clue. Above all, we want you to have fun.
