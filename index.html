Why Choose Us?

Escape Rooms are one of the fastest growing industries in the world, and they are quickly becoming homogenous.

The Secret Chambers is pushing the cutting edges of innovation to provide a more fully immersive experience than any other Escape Room in the world.  Here’s how:

  • -Completely original room concepts and stories
  • -A deeper premise, setting, and storyline
  • -Professionally trained and experienced Live action actors who interact with the customers and provide dramatic in-game events
  • -Multisensory stimulation through event-timed sound effects, setting-specific scents, and stunning visuals
  • -Mechanical moving parts that activate remotely, providing a multitude of surprises and “wow!” moments
  • -Plenty of “secret chambers” to discover, revealing previously undiscovered areas that change the dynamics of the room
  • -An overarching plot that slowly reveals new secrets as customers complete more and more rooms

FAQ

  • How many people?

    5-10 people per room per session. If you have less than 5 people give us a call and we'll try to make accommodations.

  • Special session

    Have more than 8 people? Want to participate outside our normal hours? We love special events, give us a call and we'll try to make accomodations

  • Recommended age

    We recommend ages 16 and up and all groups must have at least person over 21. No children under 6 will be admitted.

  • How hard is it?

    We provide a challenging but fair experience that requires no special skills other than teamwork. We guarantee you'll have a lot of fun.

  • When should I arrive?

    Please arrive at least 15 minutes early. If you're late and the session behind yours is booked, we must deduct the time from your session.

  • What if I get stuck?

    If you signal us, we'll give you a clue. Above all, we want you to have fun.
